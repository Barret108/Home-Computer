How to make a strawberry smoothie!

The strawberry smoothie is super easy to make and it tastes amazing,
along with all that its perfect for the warm weather!

All you will need is:

Get some milk, preferably not expired.
Now some strawberries of course!
Make sure to grab a banna or two aswell.
And finally you MUST have a blender!!!

Now how do you make it?

First make sure you have a neat space to work with!
Next you can dump some milk into a the blender, this will affect the thickness.
After that you can put some strawberries into the blender.
Almost done, next your bananas and dump them in the blender aswell.
Finally start blending and enjoy!
